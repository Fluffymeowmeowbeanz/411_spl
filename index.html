The Impact of COVID-19 on Monthly
Checkouts from the Seattle Public Library

By Tatum Lindquist, Andrew Kwon, Andrew Christensen, and Alejandro Striefel

Below, the stacked bar chart shows the total monthly checkouts between 2017 and 2022 and distinguishing them between digital versus physical items. Most clearly, there's a sharp drop in checkouts overall, starting in March 2020, corresponding with the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic. However, digital checkouts remain, and even as physical checkouts start to rise again after March 2020, they do not rise back to where they were before.
